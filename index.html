About Me

 

I'm Rie, pronounced as "ri-ye", and I'm a Malaysian Japanese-Chinese. According to the MBTI personality test, my personality is ENFJ-A, which is the same as Barack Obama's. However, I feel that I'm an ambivert. Although I enjoy meeting new people, social events, and popping my comfort bubble, there are times where I would love spending alone-time on my hobbies. These include fictional writing, listening to music, watching rom-coms of K-drama and C-drama. As for movies, my favourite are Disney and Marvel films. I love exploring all different life aspects, trying out new things and challenges.
